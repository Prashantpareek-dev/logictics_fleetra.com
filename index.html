Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FleetraShip

What is the minimum shipping rate with FleetraShip?
FleetraShip offers shipping starting at just ₹24 for domestic shipments. The exact rate depends on factors like weight, dimensions, origin, destination, and selected courier partner. Our platform automatically compares rates across multiple carriers to ensure you get the best price.
How do I integrate FleetraShip with my ecommerce store?
We offer multiple integration options including ready-made plugins for Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and other popular platforms. For custom platforms, you can use our RESTful API with comprehensive documentation. Our technical team provides free integration support to all customers.
What is the COD remittance cycle?
FleetraShip offers industry-leading COD remittance at D+7 days absolutely FREE. This means you receive your cash-on-delivery payments within 7 days of successful delivery. Express remittance options (D+3) are also available on request for faster cash flow.
Which courier partners does FleetraShip work with?
We partner with 15+ premium courier companies including BlueDart, Delhivery, FedEx, DHL, Aramex, DTDC, and many more. Our platform aggregates all these carriers, allowing you to compare rates and select the best option for each shipment based on your requirements.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! FleetraShip supports international shipping to 150+ countries through our air freight and ocean freight services. We also provide customs clearance assistance, documentation support, and end-to-end tracking for all international shipments.
Is there a minimum order volume requirement?
No, there is no minimum order volume requirement. Whether you ship 10 orders per month or 10,000, you can use FleetraShip. We serve businesses of all sizes—from startups to large enterprises. Volume-based pricing discounts are available for high-volume shippers.
How does the weight discrepancy process work?
In case of weight discrepancies, courier partners provide photo evidence of the shipment on weighing scale. You can review this evidence in your dashboard and raise a dispute if needed. Our team ensures fair resolution and works to minimize such issues through proper packaging guidelines.
What kind of customer support do you provide?
FleetraShip offers 24/7 customer support via phone, email, and live chat. Enterprise customers get dedicated account managers for personalized assistance. We also provide technical integration support, training resources, and comprehensive documentation.
